PSP Infinity 2.0

Permanent CFW for any PSP model — fast, safe, and fully reversible.

⚠️ This guide is for educational purposes only. Proceed at your own risk.

What you need

Any PSP model (1000, 2000, 3000 or Go)

A Memory Stick with at least 16MB free

A computer to download files

PSP on any official firmware

Step 1 — Check your model

Go to Settings → System Settings → System Information. Note your PSP model and firmware version. Infinity 2.0 works on all PSP models including the PSP Go.

Step 2 — Download Infinity 2.0

Download the latest Infinity 2.0 installer from the official GitHub repository. You will get an EBOOT.PBP file which is the installer.

Step 3 — Prepare your Memory Stick

Connect your PSP to your computer via USB. Navigate to PSP/GAME on your Memory Stick and create a folder called INFINITY. Place the EBOOT.PBP file inside it.

Step 4 — Run the installer

On your PSP go to Game → Memory Stick and launch Infinity 2.0. Follow the on-screen instructions. The installer will patch your firmware to enable permanent CFW on every boot.

Step 5 — Install PRO CFW

After Infinity is installed, download PRO CFW and place it on your Memory Stick the same way. Launch it from the Game menu to enable full homebrew and plugin support.

Step 6 — Done

Your PSP now boots into CFW every time. You can run homebrew, plugins, and backups from your Memory Stick without any additional steps.
