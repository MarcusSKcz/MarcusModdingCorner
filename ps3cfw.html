PS3 Custom Firmware

A complete guide to installing CFW on your PS3 Phat or Slim.

⚠️ This guide is for educational purposes only. Proceed at your own risk.

What you need

A PS3 Phat or Slim on firmware 4.91 or below

A USB drive formatted to FAT32

A computer to download files

Step 1 — Check your firmware

Go to Settings → System Settings → System Information. Check your current firmware version. If you are on 4.91 or below you are exploitable.

Step 2 — Download PS3HEN or CFW

For Slim models use PS3HEN (Homebrew Enabler). For Phat models you can install full CFW like Rebug or Cobra. Download the appropriate files from the official sources.

Step 3 — Prepare your USB

Format your USB drive to FAT32. Create a folder called PS3 and inside it another folder called UPDATE. Place the CFW .PUP file inside the UPDATE folder.

Step 4 — Install

On your PS3 go to Settings → System Update → Update via Storage Media. The console will find your CFW file and begin installation. Do not turn off the console during this process.

Step 5 — Done

After rebooting you will have CFW installed. You can now install packages, run backups and access the full potential of your PS3.
